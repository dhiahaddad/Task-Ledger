# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, safe, and constructive community. Participate with
empathy, respect differing experiences and viewpoints, give actionable feedback, and
assume good intent while remaining accountable for impact.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation,
publication of private information, and sustained disruption are unacceptable in any
project space.

Project maintainers may clarify standards, remove inappropriate material, reject
contributions, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Report conduct
concerns privately to the project maintainer. Reports will be reviewed promptly and
handled as confidentially as circumstances allow. Retaliation against a reporter is
not permitted.
